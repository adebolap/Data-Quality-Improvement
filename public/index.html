EU AI Act Transparency Notice

Simply HR uses AI for CV screening and scoring. Under the EU AI Act, automated candidate screening is classified as high-risk AI. We recommend human review of all AI-generated scores before making hiring decisions. Candidates screened using Simply HR should be informed that AI was used in the process. Security & Privacy

Your data stays yours

HR data is the most sensitive data your company holds. We treat it that way.

🔒

Encrypted in transit & at rest

All data is encrypted using TLS 1.3 in transit. MongoDB Atlas enforces encryption at rest with AES-256.

🌏

GDPR compliant

Consent tracking at field level. Withdrawal dates recorded. Configurable data retention periods per employee record.

🤖

AI data handling

CV text is sent to AI providers for screening only. We do not store CVs beyond the session and do not train models on your data.

📋

Audit-ready

All leave approvals, stage changes, and AI scores are timestamped and traceable for compliance audits.
